About us

FlatPig is a weightlifting program and lifestyle, dedicated to working out as little as possible, so people can eat whatever they want without getting too fat.

With almost 20 years of weightlifting experience, our founder has worked long and hard to find a perfect balance between strength training and eating really good food. Make no mistake about it, if you’re looking to get shredded, super strong, or super fit… you have came to the wrong place.

The FlatPig lifestyle is focused on one thing, being able to eat whatever you want, without getting fat. Hence our tagline, “Be a flat pig, not a fat pig.”

In order to have the freedom of eating whatever you want, you have to reverse engineer how that’s possible. What we’ve done is put together an incredible fitness and diet program that allows you to do just that.

Why we created this product

If you look on the internet, there are a ton of incredible fitness guru’s out there sharing valuable information. For example:

Jay Cutler: One of, if not the best bodybuilder of all time, has a show on the internet. He talks about what his routine is and what it took to become such a great body builder.

Rich Froning: Known as the fittest man on earth, has several documentaries out there that shares how hard he works.

CT Fletcher: My personal favorite, and the strongest man you’ve ever heard of.

These three guys are inspiring, talented, dedicated, etc., but they all have one thing in common, they are all extremely dedicated to being fit as a full time job. Over here at FlatPig, we’re honestly not willing to work that hard. If you are looking to work that hard, or follow a program that actually works, please go follow them.

For the other people (our research shows it’s over 85%) in the world who love to eat good food, but also like lifting weights, you’ve come to the right place.

How the program works

Why you will love this program

Over time, you will find that our lifestyle is very scalable. The workouts don’t take too long and even kids love our diet plan. That means you can live your life and be strong at the same time. You know those people who eat to live? Well, we live to eat. But at the same time, we realize how important it is to be healthy.

As of now, heart disease is responsible for more deaths in America than anything else. We don’t have any real data on this, but our assumption is that it’s due to lack of exercise and a poor diet. Which, we totally understand. There is good food everywhere. People should be able to eat a cheeseburger and fries without worrying if they’re going to clog their arteries, right?

Think about all of those diets and lame fitness companies out there. They say things like “you can eat this cake,” or “you can eat this lasagna.” You get all excited, order the food from an infomercial, and when it arrives you realize it’s the size of a golf ball…. then you get pissed.

There are also a lot of really weird and healthy diets out there that work really well. Then, you realize they taste like dog poop. Noticing this epidemic, we decided to do something about it. That’s why we finally decided to tell the world about the FlatPig lifestyle.
