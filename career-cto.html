About FlatPig

We believe that eating delicious food is more important than having a six pack...but we also believe it's important to stay healthy. That's why we've created FlatPig

The workout program and lifestyle that maximizes the ability to eat and drink whatever you want. Our current mobile app is available in the app store, and allows people to track their workouts.

However, the real mission is to build an app that allows anyone in the world to compete in this workouts.

As the Co-founder and CTO, you would receive 50% equity, depending on experience. If you’re interested in fitness, good food, and app development, this may be a great place for you. As a startup, we only have a few employees working hard to build our second version, and are looking for a more technical person to help get us there.

To apply, email us - Lift@FlatPig.com

Responsibilities

  • Work directly with the CEO to develop the iOS native app for external use.

  • Participate in full app life-cycle: concept, design, build, deploy, test and release to app store.

  • Work with team on building a great company.

  • Drive a best practices approach to the continuous improvement of our products, processes, and tools.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science and 3 or more years or Mobile App development experience.

  • Proficient in Swift

  • Experience implementing Object Oriented programming concepts.

  • Working knowledge of X-Code to build iOS applications.

  • Comfortable communicating with Slack and Trello.

  • Bonus points: Working knowledge of Android Studio to build Android applications

  • Bonus points: Experience with version control systems and Software Development Methodologies.
